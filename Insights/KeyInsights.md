## Key Insights

1. Technology emerged as the highest-performing product category in terms of sales, while Office Supplies generated the lowest sales among all categories.
2. Sales trends showed clear seasonality, with November recording the highest sales and February experiencing the lowest sales performance.
3. The West region was the top revenue-generating region with approximately $725.46K in sales, whereas the South region contributed the least with around $391.72K in sales.
4. Customer analysis revealed that Zuschuss Carroll was the highest-value customer, generating total sales of approximately $8,025.71.
5. The business achieved strong overall performance with total sales of $2.30M, total profit of $286.40K, and a healthy profit margin of 12.47%, indicating sustainable profitability.
